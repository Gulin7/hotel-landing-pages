About GT Hotel

This hotel was founded a few years ago by Gulin Tudor who wanted to attract more tourists to Gardani, the most beautiful town on earth, as well as to provide a luxurious place for the locals to relax and enjoy their time. The hotel has 5 stars and is the most luxurious hotel in Gardani. It has over 100 rooms, a spa, a gym, a shopping area, 3 restaurants, a game room, a sports area, 3 conference rooms.
